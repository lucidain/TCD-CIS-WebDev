Bosses of Terraria

Eye of Cthulu

Summoning Eye of Cthulu

Mysterious Eye summon item: Can be found in golden chests in the underground and cavern layers.

Naturally: Small chance of spawning every night when your HP reaches 200 indicated by the message, "You feel an evil presence watching you." After having defeated Eye of Cthulu, this natural spawn no longer occurs.


King Slime

Summoning King Slime

Slime Crown: Crafted with a ruby and five platinum/gold bars near an iron anvil.

Slime Rain: Small chance of occuring naturally every noon. After killing 150 slimes, King Slime spawns. After having defeated King slime for the first time, it takes 75 slimes to spawn.

Naturally: Small chance every tick to spawn in the outer thirds of the world.


Brain of Cthulu

Summoning Brain of Cthulu

Crimson Hearts: Break 3 crimson hearts with a high enough power hammer or with bombs


General Facts

  • Release Date: Terraria was released on May 16, 2011.
  • Genre: It is a 2D sandbox adventure game that combines elements of exploration, crafting, building, and combat.
  • Platforms: The game is available on multiple platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices.
  • World Generation: Each world is procedurally generated, featuring various biomes, enemies, and resources.
  • Multiplayer: Terraria supports both single-player and multiplayer modes, allowing players to collaborate or compete.
  • Updates: The game has received numerous updates, with significant content additions, including new items, bosses, and gameplay mechanics.
  • Boss Fights: Players can encounter various bosses, each with unique mechanics and challenges, contributing to the game's progression.
